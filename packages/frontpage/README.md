# Front Page

**The front page of your hedonic life.**

A hedonically-optimized content feed that surfaces news, research, tools, policies, and datasets based on YOUR assessed needs — not engagement metrics.

Current feeds optimize for clicks and rage. Front Page optimizes for hedonic relevance: content that serves the specific life domains where you're deficient, and reduces the costs blocking your access to those domains.

## How It Works

1. Import your profile from `altpath` (or set scores directly)
2. Feed items are tagged with hedonic domains (ENDS) and cost categories (MEANS)
3. The ranking engine scores content by relevance to YOUR gaps and blockers
4. Items that UNBLOCK your deficient domains rank highest

## Part of the AltPath AI Ecosystem

- **altpath** — Personal hedonic assessment (how is YOUR life?)
- **hedonics** — Shared taxonomy + fungibility calculus (the common language)
- **highnoon** — Software purpose assessment (what does this CODE do for people?)
- **mainstreet** — Policy hedonic assessment (does this POLICY serve Main Street?)
- **frontpage** — Hedonic content discovery (YOUR front page)
